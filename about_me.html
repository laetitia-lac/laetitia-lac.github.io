About me


Graduated from INSA Lyon in 2019, I am passionate about data science and software development. Eager to discover more and more, I am always learning.

I have completed different internships: starting with RoboCup Tournament in Nanjing (China), followed with a web software development project in Geneva (Switzerland) and then a data science mission in Singapore. Erasmus also gave me the opportunity to study in Trinity College of Dublin (Computer Science and Statistics Department): a year full of personal enrichment, where hiking became my new hobby.

My last internship, inside the bank Société Générale, gave me the opportunity to discover data engineering. Building robust data pipelines, useful for my teammates and at last for the customers, gave me a lot of joy. Thus, I continued my journey and worked as a Data Engineer at the e-commerce company La Redoute. During two years, I designed and deployed various pipelines on Google Cloud Platform. I am currently working for a consulting company, specialised in AI. Therefore, I design architectures to deploy production models on various cloud platforms and collaborate within amazing teams of data scientists and data analysts.
